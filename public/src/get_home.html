Introduction

Hey there! Welcome to our music API, powered and made possible by MusixMatch and Spotify Web API, created by me, @BlueG15 in collaboration with @The3dit0r.

If you're a developer or a music lover looking to bring music into your app, you're in the right place! This API combines the best of both worlds — MusixMatch for song lyrics, and Spotify for albums metadata, track info, and playlists.

Available endpoints

GET /getTrack Retrieve details for a track
GET /getAlbum Retrieve details for an album
GET /getArtist Retrieve details for an artist
GET /getPlaylist Retrieve details for a playlist
GET /getLyric Get lyrics from a specific track
